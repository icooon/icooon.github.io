OFIR LIBERMAN

The Source coder

Ofir Liberman is a Tel Aviv based artist and designer. His work bridges a wide range of disciplines: sound, graphics, coding and electronics, and focus on the relation between the human and technology in the perspective of Zen Buddhism and Shamanic practices . Ofir is a senior faculty member in Shenkar, and an expert in interaction, game design and Audio-Visual design.
